About Me

I am an instructional designer with a background in K–6 classroom teaching and computer science education. I bring a practitioner’s perspective to learning design, grounded in real classroom experience.

My work focuses on clarity, accessibility, and helping students build confidence with emerging technologies. I am particularly interested in how young learners understand complex ideas like artificial intelligence, digital citizenship, and ethical technology use.

I design learning experiences that are thoughtful, age-appropriate, and rooted in empathy for both learners and educators.
