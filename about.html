About Me

I am an instructional designer with a 15-year background in classroom teaching and computer science education. I've been creating, modifying, and implementing curriculum for over a decade.

I'm meticulous, thorough, and care deeply about the quality of my work. I'm inquisitive and passionate about keeping current with the latest technological and pedagogical advances in education, as well as developments in science more broadly.

For the past three years, I've worked extensively with AI tools—primarily ChatGPT and Claude—and have developed software in both Windows and Unix environments, mainly using Python. I bring a practitioner's perspective to learning design, grounded in real classroom experience.

I design learning experiences that are thoughtful, age-appropriate, and rooted in empathy for both learners and educators.
