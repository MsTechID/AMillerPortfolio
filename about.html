About Me

My advantage? A 15-year foundation in classroom teaching and computer science education. I've created, modified, and implemented curriculum at every stage of the learning process. That real-world experience means I design for how people actually learn, not just how content should theoretically flow.

I move fast with technology. For the past three years, I've worked extensively with AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude, and I develop custom solutions in Python across Windows and Unix environments.

AI accelerates my workflow and boosts productivity—but I bring the human skills technology can't replace: understanding what stakeholders truly need (even when they struggle to articulate it), building trust with subject matter experts, and designing with cultural sensitivity and real-world context.

What I solve are the human problems AI can't. I design learning experiences that are thoughtful, age-appropriate, and rooted in empathy for both learners and educators—bridging the gap between what technology identifies and what people actually need.
