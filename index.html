About Me

I am an instructional designer with a 15-year background in classroom teaching and computer science education. I have been creating, modifying, and implementing curriculum for over a decade.

For the past three years, I have worked extensively with artificial intelligence—primarily ChatGPT versions 3 through 5—and have developed software in both Windows and Unix environments, mainly using Python.

I bring a practitioner’s perspective to learning design, grounded in real classroom experience. I design learning experiences that are thoughtful, age-appropriate, and rooted in empathy for both learners and educators.
