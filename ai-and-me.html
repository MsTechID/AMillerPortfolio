AI & Me

AI & Me is a four-lesson instructional unit designed for upper-elementary students. The unit introduces generative artificial intelligence through realistic classroom scenarios that encourage ethical thinking, digital citizenship, and responsible technology use.

Project Goals

  • Help students understand what AI is (and what it isn’t)
  • Build awareness of safe and unsafe uses of AI tools
  • Encourage critical thinking about AI-generated content
  • Support ethical decision-making around technology

Format

Four in-person lessons combining discussion, scenarios, and guided reflection. Designed for grades 5–6.
